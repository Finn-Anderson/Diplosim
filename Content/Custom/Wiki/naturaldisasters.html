Natural disasters are a randomly generated event that can happen at any time. It is also possible multiple natural disasters can happen at one time, however, this chance is very low. The magnitude of the natural disasters along with the frequency of them can be altered at the start of the game before the egg timer is placed.

There are currently 5 natural disasters in the game with varying affects.

  • Purifier - Meteor that only harms citizens.
  • Lightning - Kills citizens, destroys trees and harms/destroys buildings.
  • Earthquakes - Can destroy buildings in range.
  • Red Sun - Harms producitivity by 50%.
  • Volcanic Eruptions - Spews out molten rock that can harm or destroy buildings.


You can protect your buildings and people from natural disasters by building natural disaster protectors.
