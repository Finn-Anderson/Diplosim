Religious buildings spread their respective religion to houses within their range. They can also hold mass for up to 100 citizens if an event is made.

The Chicken Holy Place can also be used to pray for citizens, which increases the chance of generating positive genetics for newborns. This bonus lasts a whole in-game day and is stackable.
