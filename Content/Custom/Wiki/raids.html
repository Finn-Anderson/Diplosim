There are two types of raids in this game.

The first type of raid scales based on the resources mined. So for every 200 stone, you get one stone wisp. They don't vary in ability other than just colour. This raid can spawn anywhere on the map except within 30 tiles from the egg timer.

The other type of raid is from a warring faction through the conquest map. This raid can only enter your island through the portal.

If you would like a further analysis of the conquest map, please continue to Conquest.
